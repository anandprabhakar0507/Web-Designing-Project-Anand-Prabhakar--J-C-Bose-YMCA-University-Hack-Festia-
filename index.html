IoT : Internet Of Things
Today one of the most important technology is IoT and its full form is Internet of Things.The Internet of things have many type of electronic and other devices of daily use.The motive of IoT is to connect all the daily use devices to the internet and add the Edge capabilities to the devices. Using this technology, most of the devices are getting more and more interactive because they are now made such that they can transfer and recive data from the cloud and process them as well and this is very important from tyhe aspect odf Automation.we can now a days find the IoT devices amst everywhere like in fridges , washing machines ,sensors, even in smart houses.There are many IoT kits available in market for development.Some of the popular IoT kits are based on kits bolt IoT it, arduino kits, Raspberry pi, etc and they ensure to send small packets in bytes to kilibyte rande and for this they use the MQTT (Message query Telemetry Transport) protocols unlike the other protocols used to do through which they transfer small data packets.

3D-Printing

 3D-Printing is yet another popular technique used in most of the fields to draw out the 3d-objects in the real world. 3d-models are designed in the 3d-modelling softwares like in the Autodesk fusion 360 and many other alternatives are available in market and then creating a .stl file of the model for printing in the real world.
